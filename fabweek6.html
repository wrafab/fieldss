Greetings fellow organic humanoid structures that reside on the planet effectionately named after dirt, This is what I created my sixth week of Digital Engineering

I designed my skateboard using CorelDRAW and cut out the design using the laser cutter. I then used my designed piece and glued it to six other pieces of wood. I then placed the glued pieces in an airtight bag overnight to remove air bubbles and form the shape.
